Why FP&A automation and financial systems

Most companies do financial planning in spreadsheets — manually updating data, reconciling figures, and regenerating forecasts every month. That's expensive and error-prone. Automated FP&A systems pull data from your tools (CRM, billing, payroll) and generate live forecasts, variance reports, and dashboards. Your finance team analyzes instead of collects.

The same applies to M&A. Synergy models quantify the real value of a deal — which synergies are achievable, how long they take, and the net impact to your returns. A well-built model changes how you structure the deal and what price you should pay.

Frequently asked questions

What is FP&A automation?

FP&A automation builds systems that pull financial data from your tools — CRM, billing, payroll — and automatically generate forecasts, dashboards, and variance reports. Instead of updating a spreadsheet by hand every month, your models stay live and current. It gives finance teams time to analyze instead of collect.

What is M&A synergy modeling?

M&A synergy modeling quantifies the value creation from a deal — revenue synergies (cross-sell, market expansion), cost synergies (elimination of duplicate overhead), and working capital benefits. The model shows which synergies are achievable, how long they take to realize, and the net value to your deal.

Can you build an investor-ready financial model?

Yes. I build 3-statement models (income, balance sheet, cash flow), multi-entity consolidations, and detailed pro formas for fundraising and investor underwriting. These models include sensitivity analysis, scenario planning, and the narrative deck explaining the assumptions behind the numbers.
