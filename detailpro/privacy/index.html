DetailPro Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

DetailPro ("we", "us", "the app") is a tool for auto detailing professionals to manage customers, appointments, quotes, and service packages locally on their device. This policy explains what information DetailPro collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Business data — customer names, phone numbers, email addresses, addresses, notes, appointment details, and service packages you enter.
  • Photos — before/after images you attach to jobs or service records, stored locally on your device.
  • Device ID — a local device identifier used for on-device functionality only, never transmitted to servers.
  • Subscription / purchase status — if you buy a Pro subscription, it is processed by Apple; we receive your purchase status, not your card details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core features: managing customers, appointments, quotes, and service packages entirely on your device.
  • To store your business content locally using Apple's storage framework (no cloud syncing).
  • To maintain security and prevent misuse of the app.
We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on any server. All data remains on your device. We do not sell your information, and we do not use your business data for advertising.

Data Storage & Security

All customer, appointment, quote, and photo data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage. Your data is protected by your device's passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID. We do not have access to your data, and data is never transmitted to any external server.

Third Parties

  • Apple — App Store, in-app purchases, and App Store Connect for analytics only.
  • We do not share your data with third-party advertisers, analytics services, or data brokers.

Account Deletion & Your Choices

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling DetailPro from your device. Since all data is stored locally, deletion is immediate and permanent. There is no cloud account or remote data to delete.

Analytics

DetailPro does not transmit analytics or crash reports to external servers. Limited diagnostics are stored locally on your device to help us improve the app; this data cannot be tied to your identity.

Children

DetailPro is a business tool intended for professional auto detailers aged 18 and older and is not directed to children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
