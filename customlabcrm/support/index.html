What is CustomLab CRM?

CustomLab CRM is a mobile application for sales teams to manage inquiries and leads. It syncs your inquiry data between your device and your backend API, keeping your team organized and responsive to new leads.

Key Features

  • Lead & Inquiry Management — View, filter, and manage all inquiries in one place.
  • Backend Sync — Your inquiry data automatically syncs between the app and your backend API.
  • Priority & Status Tracking — Organize inquiries by priority, status, and follow-up dates.
  • Notifications & Reminders — Get notified about new inquiries and follow-up reminders.
  • Background Sync — The app can poll your backend for new inquiries even when backgrounded.

How Do I Connect My Backend?

CustomLab CRM requires you to provide an API key to connect to your backend service. Once you add your API key, the app will sync inquiry data between your device and your backend. Your backend must be configured to accept API requests from the app.

Is My Data Secure?

Your API key is stored securely on your device. All communication with your backend uses HTTPS and Bearer token authentication. Your backend is responsible for encrypting and securing your inquiry data. We recommend using SSL/TLS, encryption at rest, and modern security practices for your backend.

Pricing

CustomLab CRM offers a free tier with core features and optional premium subscription features. Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store and your Apple ID.
