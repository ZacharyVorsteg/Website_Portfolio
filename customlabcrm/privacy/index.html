CustomLab CRM Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

CustomLab CRM ("we", "us", "the app") is a mobile lead and inquiry management application for sales teams. This policy explains what information CustomLab CRM collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • API Authentication Key — a key you provide to connect the app to your backend service, stored securely on your device.
  • Inquiry and lead data — names, emails, company names, status, priority, follow-up dates, notes, and reminders that sync between your device and your backend API.
  • User preferences — notification settings, timezone, filter preferences stored on your device.
  • Subscription / purchase status — if you purchase a subscription, it is processed by Apple; we receive your purchase status, not your card details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display, manage, and sync your inquiry and lead data between the app and your backend API.
  • To send local notifications and reminders about new inquiries and follow-up actions.
  • To provide background sync capability to pull new inquiries when the app is not active.
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse.
We do not collect, store, or sell your inquiry data. Your data syncs between your device and your own backend API — we do not operate a centralized server. We do not use your business data for advertising or third-party sharing.

Data Storage & Security

  • On Your Device — Your API key and cached inquiry data are stored securely in the app using iOS standard encryption.
  • In Transit — All communication with your backend uses HTTPS with Bearer token authentication for security.
  • Your Backend — Your backend API is responsible for encrypting and securing your inquiry data at rest and in transit. We recommend modern security practices (SSL/TLS, encryption) for your backend.

Third Parties

  • Your Backend API — the service you configure to host and manage your inquiry data.
  • Apple — App Store, in-app purchases, and push notification delivery (if enabled).
  • We do not share your data with third-party analytics, advertising services, or data brokers.

Permissions

CustomLab CRM requests the following iOS permissions:

  • Notifications — to send reminders and new inquiry alerts (optional; you can disable in Settings).
  • Background App Refresh — to poll for new inquiries when the app is backgrounded (optional; you can disable in Settings).

Data Retention & Deletion

  • While Using the App — Your inquiry data is cached locally and synced with your backend while your API key is valid.
  • After Uninstalling — All local data is removed from your device. Your backend retains data per your backend's retention policy.
  • Account Deletion — Contact your backend administrator to delete inquiry records from your backend API.

Children

CustomLab CRM is a business tool intended for sales professionals aged 18 and older and is not directed to children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
