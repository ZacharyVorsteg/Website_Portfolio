PressureWashPro Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

PressureWashPro ("we", "us", "the app") is a tool for pressure washing contractors to manage customers, jobs, quotes, and service documentation. This policy explains what information PressureWashPro collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Customer information — names, phone numbers, email addresses, residential and commercial addresses, property type, and customer notes you enter.
  • Job details — job status, pricing, service type, follow-up dates, and business notes stored locally on your device.
  • Location data — geographic coordinates and addresses associated with jobs to help track service locations.
  • Photos — before and after images you attach to jobs for documentation, stored locally on your device.
  • Contact information — if you choose to import a customer from your device's contacts.
  • Subscription / purchase status — if you purchase a subscription, it is processed by Apple; we receive your purchase status, not your card details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core features: managing customers, creating job quotes, tracking service completion, and documenting work with photos.
  • To store your business data locally on your device using Apple's secure storage framework.
  • To help you track job locations and customer information for efficient service delivery.
  • To maintain security and prevent misuse of the app.
We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on any server. All data remains on your device. We do not sell your information, and we do not use your business data for advertising.

Data Storage & Security

All customer, job, quote, and photo data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage (SwiftData). Your data is protected by your device's passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID. We do not have access to your data, and data is never transmitted to any external server.

Third Parties

  • Apple — App Store, in-app purchases, and location services.
  • We do not share your data with third-party advertisers, analytics services, or data brokers.

Location Data

PressureWashPro requests access to your device location to help you track and organize job sites. Location data is stored only on your device. You can disable location access at any time in iOS Settings without affecting other app features.

Contacts

If you choose to import a customer from your device contacts, PressureWashPro reads only the specific contact you select. We do not store, transmit, or scan your entire contacts list.

Data Deletion & Your Choices

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling PressureWashPro from your device. Since all data is stored locally, deletion is immediate and permanent. There is no cloud account or remote data to delete.

Analytics

PressureWashPro does not transmit analytics or crash reports to external servers. Limited diagnostics are stored locally on your device to help us improve the app; this data cannot be tied to your identity.

Children

PressureWashPro is a business tool intended for pressure washing contractors aged 18 and older and is not directed to children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
