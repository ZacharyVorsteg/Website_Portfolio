How commercial real estate representation works

When you need space, you typically work with a tenant rep who has relationships with landlords and access to the market. The landlord pays the commission when the lease is signed — so representation is free to tenants. A good tenant rep knows your operational requirements, negotiates better terms, and saves you time and money.
